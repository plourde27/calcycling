CAL CYCLING IS SUPPORTED BY

BAY AREA BICYCLE LAW

At Cal Cycling, we take safety seriously, so the support of Bay Area Bicycle Law is greatly appreciated. It’s comforting to know that when we’re out on the roads, there’s someone we can have in our corner if something goes wrong.

We understand how difficult the aftermath of a bicycle accident can be. All bicycle accidents are serious accidents – even with a helmet and other protective gear, being involved in a collision with a motor vehicle can be devastating. Along with the physical, emotional, and financial toll of a bicycle accident, the legal process for pursuing compensation can insert additional stress into an already frustrating situation.

At Bay Area Bicycle Law, we understand the pain and suffering you are going through. Because we are a full-service firm, we handle all the legal legwork necessary on your behalf, so that you can focus all of your energy on healing and recovery. We want to make your life easier, and we will fight to make sure you receive the help and compensation you deserve.
