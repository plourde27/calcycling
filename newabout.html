Your Home for Cycling at UC-Berkeley

The mission of Cal Cycling is to support cyclists at all levels while fielding a competitive racing team. Our club is comprised of students from all backgrounds of riding, from highly competitive racers to near-beginner riders.

Our race seasons are MTB in the Fall and road in the Spring. In between, we host group rides, social events, and other club activities.

A Highly Competitive Racing Team

We are organized as a Sport Club under Cal Sport Clubs. We race in the Western Collegiate Cycling Conference and USA Cycling. Recent results:

  • 2021 Collegiate MTB Results:
  • 4th Collegiate Nationals XC (M)
  • 4th Collegiate Nationals STXC (M)
  • 2019 Collegiate Road Results:
  • 3rd USAC Collegiate Nationals criterium (W)
  • 1st: Sea Otter Classic road race (W), Sea Otter Classic circuit race (W), UCD Criterium (W), UC-Santa Cruz Road Race (W), UCSC Circuit (W), UCSB TTT (W), UCSB Road Race (W)
  • 3rd: UCSC Road Race (M)
  • 2017 Road Collegiate Nationals: 4th TTT (W), 8th TTT (M)
  • 2016 Cal Sports Female Athlete of the Year
  • 1st 2016 Women’s XC MTB National Championship
  • 1st 2016 WCCC Women’s Individual Omnium

A Community

Most importantly, we’re a tight-knit community, a group of friends brought together by a common passion. Come here for ride partners that’ll become your friends off-the-bike.
