Alumni Success

Our alumni include Katie Hall, professional cyclist for the top-level SDWorx team and winner of the 2018 AMGEN Tour of California.

Your Home for Cycling at UC-Berkeley

The mission of Cal Cycling is to support cyclists at all levels while fielding a competitive racing team. Our club is comprised of students from all backgrounds of riding, from highly competitive racers to near-beginner riders.

Our race seasons are MTB in the Fall and road in the Spring. In between, we host group rides, social events, and other club activities.

A Community

Most importantly, we’re a tight-knit community, a group of friends brought together by a common passion. Come here for ride partners that’ll become your friends off-the-bike.
